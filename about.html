Research Profile

H. Cumaili

H. Cumaili is an independent researcher specializing in Biblical Hermeneutics, Hebrew Semantics, Comparative Abrahamic Studies, Divine Revelation, Prophetic Continuity, and Textual Criticism.

His work focuses on the continuity of revelation, theological development, semantic instability, manuscript traditions, prophetic expectation, and comparative interpretation across scripture.

Research Vision

Project Vision & Direction

The project seeks to investigate divine revelation, scriptural continuity, prophetic succession, and theological development through comparative, linguistic, historical, and textual analysis.

By combining semantic investigation, manuscript research, comparative hermeneutics, and theological analysis, the project aims to provide a structured academic framework for understanding revelation across the Abrahamic traditions.
