Research Vision

A Comparative Journey Through Revelation

This project investigates the relationship between divine revelation, scripture, prophetic continuity, theological development, and human interpretation across the Torah, Gospel, and Qur’an.

The library approaches sacred texts through historical analysis, semantic study, manuscript traditions, and comparative theological research in order to trace continuity, transformation, and religious development across history.
